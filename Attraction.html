YellowStone

The first national park of the USA. Some people think that the concept of a national park first came up during the Washburn-Doane Expedition of 1870.

Sawtooth Mountains

The Sawtooth are legendary. The mountain range central Idaho. Their jagged peaks give this range both its name and its jaw-dropping views.

Sun valley and Ketchum

Sun Valley is located on the edge of Bald Mountain and Dollar Mountain, besides skiing in the winter, it has some incredible hiking, off-roading, and mountain biking trails and fun attractions like Sun Valley Resort and Red Fish Lake.
