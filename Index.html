History of Idaho

Idaho has a Rich and Fascinating History. Diverse landscape, from the towering Rocky Mountains to its tranquil lakes, offers boundless outdoors adventures. Renowned as the "Potato State," Idaho's agricultural heritage is just one facet of its captivating history. With a history steeped in exploration and natural beauty, Idaho beckons visitors to discover its vabrant charm.

A little history of Idaho

Idaho is a state in the northwest of the U.S. that has been inhabited by native peoples for thousands of years. It was part of the Oregon Country, a disputed area between the U.S. and the British Empire, until 1846 and has become seperated states by then.
